FUTURE WORK

In the future, we hope that our technology may be used as a low energy recycling alternative. The DNA Origami is currently the highest cost component if the system – the exploration of other immobilisation surfaces with similar metabolite channeling purposes may help reduce the costs of our system.

We foresee that use of PETase and MHETase variants with enhanced stability 1 and activity 2 may improve the design of our system. The orientation of the enzymes within the nanotubule may also prove an interesting area to explore in improving reaction efficiency, but novel methods must be developed to engineer and verify orientation of strands and enzymes within DNA origami structures.
